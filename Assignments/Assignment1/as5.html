1. Movie Title 1: Vaanam (2011)
    • An engaging action-drama that masterfully interweaves the lives of five unrelated individuals.
    • Features an impressive ensemble cast including Silambarasan TR, Bharath, and Anushka Shetty.
    • Noted for its emotional depth and Yuvan Shankar Raja's memorable music.
  2. Movie Title 2: Love Today (2022)
    • A massively successful romantic comedy exploring modern relationships and trust through mobile phones.
    • A witty screenplay and directional debut for lead actor **Pradeep Ranganathan**.
    • Highly relatable contemporary premise that resonated widely with the youth.
  3. Movie Title 3: Good Day (2025)
    • A dramatic thriller focusing on a humiliated employee's long walk through the night to reclaim his dignity.
    • A plot-driven narrative with elements of dark wit and social commentary.
    • Features a compact cast including Prithiviraj Ramalingam and Myna Nandhini.
  4. Movie Title 4: Mankatha (2011)
    • A stylish action thriller/heist film centering on a corrupt cop and cricket betting money.
    • Celebrated as Ajith Kumar's 50th film, where he excelled in a negative-shaded role.
    • Directed by Venkat Prabhu, known for his signature blend of action, comedy, and suspense.
